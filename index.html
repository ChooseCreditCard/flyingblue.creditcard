Flying Blue: Miles, XP Status & Credit Cards

Flying Blue is the joint loyalty program for Air France and KLM, and a major SkyTeam ecosystem. This guide explains earning, XP status, Promo Rewards, partner redemptions and how the right credit cards help you build Flying Blue miles quickly.

Best Cards for Earning Flying Blue Miles

1. What the Flying Blue Program Is

Flying Blue is the loyalty program for Air France, KLM and several smaller partner airlines. Members earn Flying Blue Miles for redemptions and XP (Experience Points) for elite status.

The program has strong coverage across Europe, Africa, the Caribbean and long-haul routes to the Americas and Asia.

  • Miles – earned on flights, partners and credit cards.
  • XP (Status Points) – determine elite tiers.
  • Promo Rewards – monthly discounted award tickets.

2. How Flying Blue Miles Are Earned

You earn Flying Blue miles from:

  • Air France & KLM flights – miles based on ticket price and status level.
  • SkyTeam partners – including Delta, Korean Air and others.
  • Co-branded credit cards – depending on market availability.
  • Hotels, rental cars and shopping – partner earn options.
  • Promo Rewards – discounted redemptions that stretch your miles further.

Most of the earning potential outside of flying comes from heavy credit card usage and partner spending.

3. How Credit Cards Boost Flying Blue Earnings

In supported regions, Flying Blue-linked credit cards offer:

  • Base earn rates on all purchases.
  • Bonus rates on flights, dining or online spending.
  • Welcome bonuses for new cardholders.
  • XP boosts that help you climb elite tiers faster.

These cards are often the fastest way to build a meaningful Flying Blue balance without constant flying.

4. Redemption Categories in Flying Blue

Flying Blue miles can be redeemed for:

  • Air France & KLM flights (one-way or round trip).
  • Partner awards via SkyTeam and selected non-alliance carriers.
  • Cabin upgrades on eligible fares.
  • Promo Rewards offering deep monthly discounts.
  • Other travel items in select regions.

Many members chase the monthly Promo Rewards, where long-haul routes can sometimes be 25–50% off in miles.

5. Flying Blue Elite Status Tiers

Flying Blue uses XP (Experience Points) to determine status:

  • Explorer – base level.
  • Silver – priority services and some perks.
  • Gold – lounge access and strong travel benefits.
  • Platinum – top published tier with maximum recognition.

Credit cards in certain markets can award XP boosts that assist in reaching or renewing these tiers.
